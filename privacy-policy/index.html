Privacy Policy — Prime Detailing NSW

Last Updated November 2025

Prime Detailing NSW

This Privacy Policy outlines how Prime Detailing NSW (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, stores, and protects personal information provided by customers and website users. By contacting us, booking services, or using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information to provide our services:

- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Suburb / servicing location
- Vehicle details
- Booking history or service preferences
- Any information voluntarily provided through messages, forms, or bookings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used for:

- Booking and scheduling services
- Communicating with you regarding services
- Sending confirmations, reminders, and updates
- Providing quotes or follow-up service information
- Offering optional promotions or discounts if you opt-in

We do not sell or share your information for third-party marketing.

3. Payment and Security

We do not store credit card or banking details. Payments made electronically are processed through secure third-party payment gateways.

4. Data Storage

Personal information is securely stored and only accessible by authorized staff. We take reasonable measures to protect data from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access.

5. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies or analytics tools to understand visitor behaviour and improve user experience. This information is anonymous and not personally identifiable.

6. Accessing or Removing Your Information

You may request to view, update, or delete your stored personal information at any time by contacting us at: primedetailingnsw@gmail.com.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a revised date at the top of this page.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at primedetailingnsw@gmail.com
