About Us

Our Story

Prime Detailing NSW was founded by two passionate car enthusiasts from the Sydney Northern Beaches. What started as a shared love for cars has grown into a small, dedicated business focused on delivering perfection in every detail. We believe every car deserves to look its best, and we personally ensure that each vehicle we work on shines inside and out, just like it rolled straight out of the showroom. With our hands on approach, every detail matters to us and it will show in your car.

Why Choose Us

We may be a small team, but that’s our advantage. At Prime Detailing NSW, you get personal, professional care from people who genuinely care about your car. Our mobile detailing service brings high-quality cleaning right to your doorstep, saving you time while enhancing your car’s appearance and value. Every vehicle receives meticulous attention, ensuring a flawless finish that preserves that “new car” feeling. With us, your car isn’t just detailed it’s treated with the care it deserves.
